Protocols Management System

In the year 2015, I Designed and Developed the Protocols Management System to help in the management of KEMRI-RCTP Study Protocols.


The system has functionalities that enables the organization to keep track of study protocols application and renewal processes. It includes an automated Email Reminder that sends alert emails to Principal Investigators whose protocols are about to expire which has helped the organization realize 100% zero delays in the protocol renewal and submission processes.


The system is still in use up to date.
