Cancer Tracking System

CATSystem is a collaboration between Kenya Medical Research Institute (KEMRI), Global Health Innovation Fund (GHI) and the University of Kansas Medical Center (KUMC).


I currently work on this project together with a team of medical researchers from the United States and Kenya with an aim to produce a robust clinical management tool that will strengthen Cervical Cancer Screening and Treatment procedures and processes in Kenya and the rest of Africa.


View our progress at http://koyowe.github.io/catsystem
